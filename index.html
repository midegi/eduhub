A little bit of everything

Khan Academy

Khan Academy is a non-profit that aims to provide free, world-class education for anyone, anywhere.

Edx

edX is the trusted platform for education and learning. Founded by Harvard and MIT, edX is home to more than 20 million learners.

Open Learn

OpenLearn is a free learning platform, delivered by The Open University to support the wellbeing of the community.

MIT Open Course Ware

MIT OpenCourseWare is a web-based publication of virtually all MIT course content. OCW is open and available to the world.

Coursera

Coursera is a freemium platform that provides some free MOOC, partnering with top universities and organizations.

Lynda

Lynda.com is a freemium online learning platform that helps anyone learn various skills to tackle personal and professional goals.

Udacity

Udacity is another freemium online education provider that offers online courses, with a stronger focus on job training.

Wikipedia

Wikipedia is a free multilingual online encyclopedia, based on open collaboration through a wiki-based content editing system.

Open Culture

Open Culture is a platform that offers free audiobooks, free online courses, free movies, free language lessons, free ebooks and other enriching content.

TED-Ed

Ted-Ed is a Youtube channel that provides high-quality content to spark and celebrate the ideas of teachers and students around the world.

Kurzgesagt

Kurzgesagt is a Youtube channel that produces animated educational content about sciences and humanities subjects.

Crash Course

Crash Course is a Youtube channel that aims to divulge crash courses on various subjects.

VSauce

Michael Stevens, aka Vsauce, provides high-quality educational content about philosophy, sciences, technology, culture and so on.

Big think

Big Think is a Youtube channel that offers different lessons from the world's greatest thinkers.

Project Gutenberg

Project Gutenberg was the first website to provide free eBooks. It’s a great resource for free non-educational and educational ebooks.

Open Stax

Open Stax is a non-profit platform that provides free access to high-quality textbooks for students.

LibriVox

LibriVox is a digital library that makes many public domain free audiobook available.

Pdf Drive

PDF Drive is a free search engine that helps you to search, preview and download millions of free books.

CGP Grey

CGP Grey is a youtube channel that uploads animated, educational content about various arguments.

Sciences

Catalogue of life

The catalogue of life is a website that provides a wide database about the world's known species of animals, plants, fungi and micro-organisms.

Brain Facts

Brain Facts is a website born by a public information initiative dedicated to advancing brain research.

Erowid

Erowid is an amazing website by a non-profit educational organization that provides information about psychoactive plants and chemicals.

National Geographic

National Geographic’s Youtube channel offers high-quality content about science, exploration, and adventure.

Animal Planet

Animal Planet is a youtube channel that immerses the viewers in the full range of life in the animal kingdom.

Atlas Pro

Atlas Pro is a youtube channel that creates videos to learn more about the Earth through geography, geology, biology, and ecology.

Astrum

How big is the Solar System? What actually orbits our Sun? Why should I even be interested in space?If you are interested in these topics, this channel is for you.

PBS Space Time

Space Time is a youtube channel explores everything about space, astrophysics and anything about Earth-related topics.

Scishow

SciShow is a science-related youtube channel that tries to explain how our world works.

Science Channel

Science Channel is a youtube channel that offers videos about space, scientific exploration, technology, earth and more.

Veritasium

Veritasium is a youtube channel about science and engineering, that offers videos featuring experiments, expert interviews and more.

AsapSCIENCE

AsapSCIENCE is a YouTube channel that offers channel produces weekly videos that touch on many different topics of science.

Physics Girl

Physics Girl is a YouTube channel about physical sciences that shows experiments, demonstrations, and cool new discoveries.

minutephysics

minutephysics is a youtube channel that tries to explain physic in an easy and understandable way.

It's Okay To Be Smart

Joe Hanson’s It's Okay to Be Smart is a youtube channel that tries to explain how our universe works with cool videos.

Looking Glass Universe

Looking Glass Universe creates various videos about maths and physics on her youtube channel.

Humanities

Poem Hunter

Poemhunter is a website that collects different poems of a lot of authors.

Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy

Stanford Encyclopedia of Philosophy is a digital philosophical encyclopedia, by Stanford University.

The History of philosophy

This website is a summary of the history of (Western) philosophy that shows the connections between some of the key ideas of various philosophers.

Wiktionary

Wiktionary is a free online multilingual dictionary born by a collaborative project on the internet.

Etymonline

The Online Etymology Dictionary collects explanations of what English words meant and how they sounded 600 or 2,000 years ago.

Origin Of Everything

Origin of Everything is a Youtube show about under told history and culture hosted by Danielle Bainbridge.

OverSimplified

OverSimplified is a Youtube Channel that tries to explain history in an oversimplified way.

Montemayor

Montemayor is a Youtube Channel about military history that focuses on explaining not-so-well known battles as well as famous ones, from the gunpowder age to today.

Programming

The Odin Project

The Odin Project is a free open source project that collects everything that is needed to know to become a web developer.

HTML Dog

HTML Dog is a website that includes various Tutorial about HTML, CSS and Javascript.

Traversy Media

Traversy Media is an amazing Youtube channel about Web Developing. It includes various crash courses and even various tutorials related to creating full websites.

SoloLearn

SoloLearn is an online and mobile learning platform that offers free coding classes in 13 different programming disciplines.

FreeCodeCamp

Free Code Camp is an open source community that helps people learn to code and get a job as a developer.

CodeWars

Codewars is an educational community about programming. On the platform, the developers train themselves by completing different programming challenges called kata.

Stack Overflow

StackOverflow is a platform where students and professionals could post questions and receive answers about programming.

Stack Exchange

Stack Exchange is a network of Q&A websites on topics in diverse fields, each site covering a specific topic.

Leetcode

LeetCode is a freemium website that aims to provide hands-on training on real coding interview questions.

Codeacademy

CodeAcademy is a freemium platform that provides various free courses about programming.

Language

Duolingo

Duolingo is a free language-learning website where you can learn languages by playing.

Clozemaster

Clozemaster is gamified language learning website that provides a more sentence based and contextual learning experience to complement other language learning apps.

Memrise

Memrise is a free language learning website that tries to focus on a combination of memorizing techniques and entertaining materials.

Mango Languages

Mango Languages is a freemium online language learning resource for individuals and organizations around the world.

Busuu

Busuu is a freemium social network that aims to make you learn a new language, by talking with native speakers.

HelloTalk

HelloTalk is a freemium app that makes you learn a language by chatting with native speakers.

Math

Khan Academy

Khan Academy is a non-profit that aims to provide free, world-class education for anyone, anywhere. It's also a great resource for math-learning.

PatrickJMT

PatrickJMT is a youtube channel that creates mathematics videos that are especially useful to help students to pass their classes.

Numberphile

Numberphile is a Youtube channel related to numbers and mathematics. The stars of the show include mathematicians and other guests from around the world.

MindYourDecisions

MindYourDecisions is a Youtube channel that makes videos about riddles, logic puzzles, viral math problems, and math topics.

Mathispower4u

Mathispower4u is a Youtube channel that produces math tutorials, from basic arithmetic through calculus III and beyond.

3Blue1Brown

3blue1brown is a Youtube channel that combines math and entertainment. This channel aims to make difficult problems simpler with changes in perspective.

NancyPi

NancyPI is a Youtube Channel that makes video about algebra, trigonometry, and calculus.

Music

Lightnote

Lighnote is a free interactive web-app to learn the basics of music theory.

Justin Guitar

Justinguitar is a free website that offers over 1000 free lessons and songs about guitar.

Teoría

teoria.com is a free website dedicated to the study and practice of music theory.

Songsterr

Songsterr is an archive of guitar, bass and drum tabs. It is collaboratively built and maintained by your fellow music lovers.

Musictheory.net

Musictheory is a website that offers various lessons, exercises and tools about music theory.

The Shed

The Shed is a website that offers different music education resources.

Other

Lichess

lichess.org is a completely free, open-source chess platform - exclusively powered by volunteers and donations.

Our Planet

Our Planet is the website from the Netflix original documentary series. It contains a lot of useful resources about climate changes.

Atlas Obscura

Atlas Obscura is a website that offers a lot of information about the world’s hidden wonders.

Drawabox

Drawabox is a website that offers various resources that are useful to learn how to draw.

StudySmarter

StudySmarter is an online platform that offers everything you need for your studies: Learning plans, mind maps, flashcards and summaries all in one platform.

Explorabl.es

Explorable Explanations is a platform for learning by playing with different mini games about various subjects.

Makezine

Make is a community that aims to provide various DIY guides about different projects.

instructables

Instructables is a community for people where you can learn how to make things.

iFixit

iFixit is a wiki-based site that teaches people how to fix almost anything.
